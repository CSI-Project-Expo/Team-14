# Academic Consequence Engine

Simulate your semester before you ruin it.

## Problem Statement
Students often realize attendance shortages and CGPA risks only after it is too late. Existing systems show past records but do not help predict future consequences.

## Solution
Academic Consequence Engine helps students simulate academic decisions and see their impact before acting.
